Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

This website describes the privacy practices for a private media server operated for invited friends and family.

Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (only if you choose to receive SMS notifications)
  • Media requests submitted through Jellyseerr

How We Use Information

Information is used only to:

  • Provide access to the media server.
  • Process media requests.
  • Notify users when requested media becomes available.
  • Respond to support requests.

SMS Messaging

SMS notifications are only sent to users who voluntarily provide their phone number and request text notifications.

Message frequency varies based on your requests. Message and data rates may apply.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Mobile opt-in data and consent will never be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Data Security

Reasonable measures are used to protect account information and personal data.

Contact

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy may be directed to the server administrator.
